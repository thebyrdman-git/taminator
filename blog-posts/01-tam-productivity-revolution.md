## The Automation Breakthrough

That's when I decided to build something revolutionary: **a complete RFE automation platform** that would transform not just my workflow, but potentially every TAM's productivity.

Working with our internal PAI (Personal AI Infrastructure) team, we created a system that:

- **Discovers cases automatically** using rhcase integration
- **Enriches with real-time JIRA status** 
- **Generates professional portal content** with customer-specific templates
- **Updates customer portals safely** with zero email notifications
- **Monitors everything** with comprehensive alerting

The result? **One command replaces 3 hours of manual work.**

```bash

```

That's it. 30 seconds later, Wells Fargo has a completely updated, professionally formatted RFE status page with 100% accurate case discovery and real-time JIRA information.
