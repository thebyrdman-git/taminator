### 1. **Production Incident Response** 
**Problem**: Single point of failure in event processing delays critical automated responses
**Customer Impact**: 
- Delayed security incident remediation (MTTR increases 300-500%)
- Failed SLA compliance during outages
- Manual intervention required during automation system failures

**Value with HA**:
- Continuous event processing during component failures

### 2. **Compliance and Auditing**
**Problem**: Automation gaps create compliance violations and audit findings
**Customer Impact**:
- SOC 2, PCI-DSS, HIPAA violations due to missing automated controls
- Failed audit findings for continuous monitoring gaps
- Regulatory fines and remediation costs ($100K-$5M typical range)

**Value with HA**:
- Continuous compliance monitoring without gaps
- Audit trail maintained through failover scenarios  
- Regulatory confidence in automated control reliability

### 3. **Business Continuity Planning**
**Problem**: Event-driven automation is critical infrastructure requiring 99.9%+ uptime
**Customer Impact**:
- Business process disruption during maintenance windows
- Revenue loss during automated system outages
- Customer experience degradation from manual processes

**Value with HA**:
- Zero-downtime maintenance and upgrades
- Business process continuity during infrastructure failures
- Consistent customer experience through automation reliability
