# What's new with Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform 2.6

**Event Type:** Webinar  
**Live Event Date:** Wednesday, October 15, 2025, at 01:00 PM EST  
**On-Demand Availability:** One year after live event  
**Source:** [Red Hat Webinar](https://www.redhat.com/en/events/webinar/whats-new-red-hat-ansible-automation-platform-2.6?blaid=7931913)

## Overview

The world of IT is changing fast, and your team needs a trusted, resilient foundation to keep pace. Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 delivers new features, platform enhancements, and strategic integrations to help you meet these challenges head-on.

Join us for this webinar as we reveal how the latest version of Ansible Automation Platform helps you unlock more value from your automation with a new dashboard, achieve new levels of scale with a self-service portal, and operate more efficiently with the new Ansible Lightspeed intelligent assistant. We'll also provide a clear path forward for customers looking to upgrade and take advantage of these powerful new capabilities.

## Key Topics Covered

### 1. Building Your Future
We will show how Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 helps you build a strong, resilient foundation for the next generation of IT.

### 2. Automate Smarter and Faster
This release is all about helping you unlock more value and achieve new levels of scale. Three major new features:

#### New Analytics Dashboard
- See the value of your automation in real-time
- Understand your ROI
- Track your time savings
- Make smarter, data-driven decisions

#### Self-Service Portal
- Empower more people to use automation
- Securely share automation with other teams
- Make it easy for varying personas to get things done

#### AI Assistant (Ansible Lightspeed)
- Get on-demand expertise
- Powered by generative AI
- Helps with onboarding, learning, and day-to-day tasks

### 3. Easy Upgrades and More Integrations
- Platform's refreshed design
- New integrations with leading cloud and infrastructure partners
- Simplified upgrade process
- Confident upgrading to take advantage of new capabilities
