# Feedback & Backlog

## How to give feedback

- **GitLab issues (coworkers):** https://gitlab.cee.redhat.com/jbyrd/taminator/-/issues — Bug reports, feature requests, “I wish it did X.” Official place for Red Hat feedback; GitLab only for now.
- **Direct:** jbyrd@redhat.com (or your preferred channel).
- **Usage:** What you run, which customers, what you do with the report after (copy/paste, post manually, etc.).
